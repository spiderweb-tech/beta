About Us



Biosetup Life sciences is a hub for scientific research and learning. It is approved by ISO 9001:2015 Certified Organization and registered under MSME, Govt. of India. BSL comprises a multidisciplinary team of faculty and research associates.

Vision- To provide support to the aspiring students in the field of Life Sciences. To provide skill development and provide services to manufacturing companies involved in the field of Lifescience and involved in biotech research services.

Mission- To enhance the knowledge in basic and applied life sciences fields. To increase employment opportunities for science graduates

Bioentrepreneurship

Bioentrepreneurship, the fusion of science and entrepreneurship. This involves experimentation, achieving a goal, making high-risk decisions and attempting something novel / innovative. Today we are moving forward in every field of science and soon a time will come when there will be more demand for highly effective and better medical care, producing better crops,energy sources etc. As bioentrepreneurs, you need to come forward and tackle these changes and challenges. Bioentrepreneurs can provide service for better lifestyle and also helps to generates the employment make the the "Make in India" campaign come true..
